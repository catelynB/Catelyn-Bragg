Is Quo Vadis for you?

Quo Vadis is a homeschool high school speech and debate club.We meet once a week on Mondays at faith communinty center. If your student likes compition, research, preforming and wants to be streched academically Quo Vadis might be for you! Faith Community Center hosts Quo Vadis Speech and Debate Club. Quo Vaids hosts a variety of activities which help the whole family work on their communication skills, and also a Gym & Swim program for younger children.

Quo Vadis Mission Statement

To assist families in developing skills in their children in order that they can be effective ambassadors for Christ in this lost world. To fulfill this mission we use a two-fold approach - competition and community communication.
